Kart Deko 3000

About

Kart deko was a project that I was part of as leader of a small programming team for "GameLab". In this game the player can use a car to compete either against time or against 1 or 3 other people.

Project info

Role: Gameplay programmer
Team Size: 12
Duration: 6 months
Engine: Unity (C#)

Development

My responsabilities during this project were:
- Developed a kart controller with a strong focus on karting style, implementing features such as a steering, gravity, drift, and airborne system.
- Developed three different game modes: player vs. time (1vtime), player vs. player (1v1), and a battle royale mode.
- Implemented a power-up system featuring two power-ups: a speed boost and a grappling hook.
- Implemented an audio system responsible for all audio
- Implemented a manager that controls the game modes and player positioning.
