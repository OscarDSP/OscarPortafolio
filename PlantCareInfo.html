Plant Care

About

As part of my thesis, I had to develop a procedural algorithm for the growth of plants. In order to make that possible, I developed a videogame which its main mechanic had to do with said algorithm. Plant Care is a game where the player has to take care of plants (which will grow procedurally) and once the plants are ready, the player will be able to sell them.

Project info

Role: Game programmer & designer
Team Size: 1
Duration: 1 year
Engine: Unity (C#)

Development

My responsabilities during this project were:
- Designed and Implemented a simple game flow for the game.
- Developed the game system for all the scenes in the game.
- Developed the game manager resposible for the correct managment of the game system and flow.
- Developed the audio system resposible for the correct managment of the background music and sound effects in the game.
- Developed the procedural algorithm for the growth of plants. This algorithm will generate the most important parts of a plant, stem, branches, leaves and fruits and no plant will be the same as the previous one.
I also wanted to optimize the algorithm so players can generate big plants in short periods of time. For that I avoided the use of recursion and I used "BFS" for the creation and connection of branches.
- Developed the AI ​​for the plant, so it can request different items depending on what is missing.
- Developed the AI for the customers, so they can enter the store and request plants with different characteristics. Once the player gives a plant to the customers, they will decide the adequate amount of money to give the player for the plant.
