Endless Jumping

About

One of my first games published on the web. Endless Jumping was made to experiment with a timed difficulty system. In this game, the player has to jump between the floor and the ceiling to avoid the obstacles and survive as much as the player can

Project info

Role: Game programmer & designer
Team Size: 1
Duration: 2 weeks
Engine: Unity (C#)

Development

My responsabilities during this project were:
- Design and development of the game system.
- Implemented the audio for the game
- Implemented the animations for the player and the enemies
- Developed a difficulty system over time where among all the values ​​to change, the most important are the speed and number of obstacles - Developed the main Mechanic where the player can go from the ceiling to the floor and vice versa
