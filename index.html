About me

Hello, I'm Oscar Dominguez, a 22-year-old gameplay programmer with 4 years of dedicated experience in the exciting realm of video game development. Armed with a degree in Computer Games and Programming Skills from SAE Institute, I've honed my expertise across various platforms and programming languages, with a special affinity for C++ and C#. My true passion lies in crafting interactive, captivating gaming experiences that leave players enthralled.

I've always seen myself as a problem solver, continuously seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and elevate my skill set. I take immense pride in my work, striving to create games that not only play exceptionally but also feature clean, well-organized code that's a joy to read. I thrive on the challenges that game development brings, often finding it hard to let go until I've cracked the most complex coding puzzles. I'm eagerly looking forward to lending my technical prowess and creative touch to the next thrilling project in the ever-evolving world of video game development.
