Village Invader

About

"Village Invader was developed for the final project of an artificial intelligence class. This is a strategy game where the player can deploy units to defend and attack the opponent."

Project info

Role: Game programmer & designer
Team Size: 1
Duration: 6 months
Engine: Unity (C#)

Development

My responsabilities during this project were:
- Design and development of the game system.
- Implemented the audio for the game
- Implemented the animations for the game
- Developed Nine characters, each with its own artificial intelligence, perception system, and behaviors.
- An AI was created that is capable of challenging the player, utilizing the player's actions to make strategic decisions.
